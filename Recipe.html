Chocolate Chipped Cookie Cake

  • Time: 3.5-4 hours
  • Serves: cake bowl
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees farenheit
  2. Sift dry ingredients into large bowl, and use one thats at least 2 inches deep and 11 inch ovenproof skillet.

  3. In another large bowl, or in the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment, add the butter and the sugars. Mix just until the butter and sugars are blended. Use a spatula to scrape down the sides of the bowl along the way. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing until each is combined. Mix in the vanilla. Add the flour mixture to the bowl and blend on low speed until the flour is barely combined, about 30 seconds. Scrape down the sides and bottom of the bowl.

  4. Add most chocolate to the batter and mix until evenly incorporated.
  5. Bake for 35-45 minutes or until dough is deep golden brown
